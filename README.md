## 👋 About Me

I'm **David Cruz Anaya** — a Senior Software Engineer with **10+ years** of Android development experience, born in Spain 🇪🇸 and based in London 🇬🇧. I run **[Vectencia Ltd](https://vectencia.com)**, a technology consultancy specialising in mobile engineering, product ventures, and technical training.

I'm also the author of **📖 "You Are Not That Important"** — available in [English](https://amzn.eu/d/0dllIl4D) and [Spanish](https://amzn.eu/d/01O3UbeF), and an educator with **20,000+ students** on Udemy.

- 🏢 Currently contracting as a **Staff Android Engineer**
- 🚀 Building **[Paglipat.com](https://www.paglipat.com)** — a travel metasearch platform
- ✍️ Writing technical content at **[davthecoder.com](https://www.davthecoder.com)**
- 🎓 Teaching Kotlin & Android on **[Udemy](https://www.udemy.com/user/davidcruzanayadacran/)**
- 🌍 Dual UK-Spanish citizen exploring the digital nomad lifestyle
