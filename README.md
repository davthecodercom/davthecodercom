## ⚡ About Me

I'm **David Cruz Anaya** — an Android & Kotlin Multiplatform specialist with **12+ years** in mobile engineering. Born in Spain 🇪🇸, based in London 🇬🇧.

I'm the **Founder & Principal Architect** at **[Vectencia Ltd](https://vectencia.com)** — a technology consultancy focused on high-performance software architecture for enterprise and FinTech clients.

I'm also the author of **"You Are Not That Important"** — available in [English](https://amzn.eu/d/0dllIl4D) and [Spanish](https://amzn.eu/d/01O3UbeF), and an educator with **20,000+ students** on Udemy.
